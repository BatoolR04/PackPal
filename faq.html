Frequently Asked Questions

What is PackPal?

PackPal is a smart packing list generator and trip planner. It helps you prepare for your trip by suggesting items based on your destination and type of travel.

Do I need to create an account?

You can preview packing lists without logging in, but registering allows you to save your trips, customize lists, and access your data later.

Can I customize my packing list?

Yes! After generating your list, you can add or remove items, check off packed items, and track your progress with a pie chart.

Is PackPal mobile-friendly?

Yes, PackPal is responsive and works well on both desktop and mobile devices.

Is my data secure?

Yes, all data is stored securely in our database and only accessible to you or site administrators.
